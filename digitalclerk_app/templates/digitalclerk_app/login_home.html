Welcome to Digital Clerk.

Digital Clerk is a tool that scales up office hours for computer science learning.

How does Digital Clerk work?

Students can view a list of office hours available for a certain module they are enrolled into which they can then choose one to attend.

Students submits a request for a question or problem and is added into a vitual queue until a staff member is ready to see them.

Help staff manages the list of questions raised by students which is sorted according to the time the question was raised.

Help staff are encouraged to provide feedback and "next steps" suggestions for students after addressing their questions.

Making the most out of office hours.

  • Students must formulate their questions in detail before asking for help.
  • Stuents can see how many request are ahead of them ensuring that they are ready when a help staff is ready to see them.
  • When a help staff is ready to meet a student, an email notification is sent to the student.
  • Help staff can choose to "close" a question immediately without seeing them if question raised is trivial or student did not provide sufficient information.
  • The number of students enrolled in a module is recorded and displayed which can provide information about how many students utilizes office hours.
